About Us

Our Origins: A Dream with Paw Prints The story of Lyfter Pet Shop began in 2008, in a small 30-square-meter storefront in the heart of the San Benito neighborhood. Its founder, Carlos Lyfter, a passionate animal rescuer, noticed that his community struggled to find products that combined high nutritional quality with prices that allowed every family to care for their pets as they deserved. What started with just two shelves of organic food and a single grooming tub quickly grew. Thanks to "word of mouth" recommendations from neighbors who saw Carlos not just as a shopkeeper, but as an ally in their companions' well-being, the business began to flourish.

Evolution and Growth: By 2015, Lyfter Pet Shop took a major leap in technology and logistics. We transitioned from a local shop to launching our online store, allowing our philosophy of "Happy Pets, Peaceful Families" to reach every corner of the country. In 2021, we celebrated the opening of our Integrated Wellness Center. Beyond just products, we incorporated professional grooming services, personalized nutritional consulting, and canine and feline behavior workshops.

Our Core Philosophy: At Lyfter Pet Shop, we don’t see animals as just pets; they are fundamental members of the family unit. Our mission is built on three unbreakable pillars:

🐶 Uncompromising Quality: We select every product under strict safety and health standards. If it’s not good enough for our own animals, it doesn’t make it to our shelves.
🐶 Real Affordability: We believe that loving an animal shouldn't be dictated by a budget. We work directly with suppliers to ensure the best quality-price ratio on the market.
🐶 Social Commitment: We donate 5% of our monthly sales to local shelters and sterilization campaigns, giving back to the community of rescued animals that gives us so much joy.

Looking Toward the Future: Today, with over 15 years of experience, our goal remains the same as day one: to create an environment where the health and happiness of your pets are the top priority. We are constantly innovating and seeking new sustainable and eco-friendly products to ensure a greener planet for future generations of owners and pets alike. At Lyfter Pet Shop, we walk by your side—paw after paw.
